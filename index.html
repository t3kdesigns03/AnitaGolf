Welcome to Crestwood Hills

Golf the Way It Was Meant to Be Played

Crestwood Hills Golf Course is a beautiful 40+ year, 18-hole public course nestled in the rolling hills of Anita, Iowa. Located alongside Lake Anita State Park, we offer some of the most scenic fairways you'll find anywhere in the state.

The lake and rolling Iowa countryside are part of the experience. Some of our fairways run right alongside Lake Anita — making for a breathtaking round every time you play.

On the Course

Course Rules

Please review our local rules before teeing off. The course plays by USGA rules except where noted below.

1

Pace of Play

9 holes should be played in approximately 2 hours. Please keep pace with the group ahead. Slow players must allow faster groups to play through.

2

Cart Rules

Golf carts must remain on the cart path within 30 yards of greens. 90-degree rule is in effect unless otherwise posted. Keep carts away from tees and greens.

3

Divots & Ball Marks

Replace your divots with the provided sand/seed mix. Repair all ball marks on greens — yours and others. This keeps the course great for everyone.

4

Out of Bounds & Hazards

White stakes mark out of bounds. Red stakes indicate lateral water hazards. Yellow stakes indicate water hazards. All USGA rules apply unless local signage states otherwise.

5

Dress Code

Golf attire is expected on the course and in the clubhouse. Collared shirts, golf slacks, or shorts are appropriate. No cutoffs, swimwear, or bare feet.

6

Respect Other Golfers

Please be courteous on the course. Cell phone conversations should be kept brief and quiet. Do not disturb others while addressing or playing their shots.

7

Lightning Policy

When the horn sounds, all play must stop immediately. Golfers should seek shelter in the clubhouse or their vehicle. Play resumes only when the all-clear horn sounds. Never seek shelter under trees during a storm.
